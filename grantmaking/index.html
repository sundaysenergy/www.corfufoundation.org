The Corfu Foundation reviews proposals by invitation only. Grants support preventive, holistic approaches that target the following areas of interest:

  • Spiritual expansion
  • Ecological sustainability
  • Mind/body well-being
  • Leadership

Our goal is to serve a catalytic role in funding seed projects and innovative ideas that may fall outside the parameters of mainstream philanthropy.

Support is provided in the form of:

  • Project grants
  • Fellowship grants for individuals
  • Matching grants

The Foundation funds research, workshops, conferences, and the production of documentaries and books that align with its core areas of interest. We encourage those who have become successful with the support of the Corfu Foundation to continue in the spirit of generosity.

Conscious Media Program

The prevalence of media in today’s society offers an extraordinary opportunity to educate and motivate audiences to take action around critical global issues. The Foundation supports filmmakers whose work offers new insights into an issue or challenge and has the potential to spark broad-based dialogue and action. Special attention is given to providing seed funding for projects that might otherwise have difficulty securing support and that explore topics overlooked by the mainstream media.
